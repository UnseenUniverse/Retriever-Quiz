Golden Retrievers are a great fit for you!

Golden Retrievers have medium length, wavy, double coat consisting of a water repellent top coat and a soft undercoat. They shed year round but more during warm weather. They are slightly smaller than Labradors and come in a color range of cream to red. Their medium energy level makes them great for families with older children (and other pets!). Goldens are known to be very kind and therapeutic, they are often therapy dogs for autistic children. All dogs are different even dogs of the same breed. Take the time to get to know a dog before adopting!

Labrador Retrievers have short, straight and very dense coat that consists of a water resistant undercoat. They shed year round but more during warm weather. They are slightly bigger than Goldens and come in many colors the most common being Black, Gold and, Brown.
